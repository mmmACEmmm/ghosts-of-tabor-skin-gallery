What Taboreo stores

When you sign in, Taboreo uses Discord through Supabase Auth to identify your account. The site may store your Discord display name, avatar, uploaded preview images, trade listing text, and optional trade thumbnails.

What is public

Approved gallery previews are public. Market listings are public by design and may show your Discord display name, optional trader tag, listing details, and thumbnail.

What admins can see

Admin accounts can review preview submissions and moderate site content. They can also review trade listings if needed for safety or rule enforcement.

Trading rules

Real-money trading is not allowed on Taboreo. Listings must stay limited to in-game items and in-game Korunas. If you post or coordinate a listing, use the Operational Raid Brigade Discord for follow-up.
