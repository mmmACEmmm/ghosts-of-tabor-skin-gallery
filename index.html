SKINNING SYSTEM

How skins work in Ghosts of Tabor

The skinning system was added on December 2, 2024. It lets players customize the appearance of certain gear and bring those items into raids. Skins can be earned through missions, Twitch drops, seasonal play, or by purchasing specific DLCs or game editions.

Station Layout

The skinning station is located in the armory section of your bunker. It has three main areas:

  • On the left is a stand for vests, helmets, and backpacks.
  • In the center is the main console, where you select skins and pick up the skinning gun.
  • On the right is a stand for items like weapons, magazines, and face shields.

How Persistence Works

Once an item is skinned, it stays that way unless you manually reset it. If another player takes the item out of a raid, the skin does not reset.

Some skins are no longer obtainable, such as limited-time game edition rewards or certain Twitch drop skins like RFCP. The BOA skin is a special skin awarded to players who become a BOA, which requires going through an application process.

Apply a Skin

  1. Place the item on the correct stand.
  2. Select the skin on the console.
  3. Grab the skinning gun and spray the item.
  4. Use the marks shown on the console and spray those spots on the item, usually 1 to 3 places.
  5. Once all marks are hit, the item will be fully skinned.

Remove a Skin

  1. Place the item on the correct stand.
  2. Go to the console.
  3. Press the reset button in the top-right corner.
  4. This will remove the skin from the item.
